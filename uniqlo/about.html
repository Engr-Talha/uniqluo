Careers

I'm Maha Zaheer Khan, young multidisciplinary fashion designer with a passion for creativity and education living in Pakistan. I grew up in Islamabad and graduate with the Bachelor degree in Fashion & Design in 2016. In the last year of my degree I kickstarted my career as a creative fashion designer with a renowned brand Mohtarram Menswear in Islamabad. After six months, I decided to switch gears and pursue my love for fashion education. That's when I joined TMUC Pakistan, a leading transnational education provider. In my 8 years of this journey, I honed my skills massively which landed me to get my current role as a Program Manager for the Fashion and textile department at Head Office TMUC Pakistan. Throughout my 8-year career, I've had the pleasure of organizing fashion shows and fashion textile exhibitions, providing a platform for students to showcase their creativity and talent while fostering industry connections. I ensure the delivery of high-quality coursework and maintain the integrity of the assessment process of the Edexcel BTEC Qualification across TMUC's nationwide campuses as a Lead Internal Verifier. I'm truly passionate about nurturing the next generation of fashion professionals and maintaining the highest standards of education and assessment. Join me on this exciting journey as we explore the world of fashion together!
